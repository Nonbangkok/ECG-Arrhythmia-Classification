Class 1: Supraventricular Premature Beat

Supraventricular premature beats are early heartbeats originating above the ventricles (in the atria or AV node). They are also called premature atrial contractions (PACs).

  • May cause a feeling of skipped or extra beats
  • Usually benign and common, especially with stress, caffeine, or fatigue
  • Rarely require treatment unless symptomatic or frequent

Frequent supraventricular premature beats may indicate underlying heart or metabolic conditions and should be evaluated by a physician.
