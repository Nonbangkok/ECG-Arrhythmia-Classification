Class 0: Normal Heart Rhythm

Normal heart rhythm (Sinus Rhythm) is the natural rhythm of a healthy heart. It means the electrical impulses are being generated and conducted properly, resulting in a regular and coordinated heartbeat.

  • Heart rate: 60-100 beats per minute (in adults)
  • Regular rhythm and intervals
  • No abnormal premature beats

Normal rhythm is a sign of a healthy heart and is the reference for comparison with arrhythmias.
